Who Am I?

My name is Kevin Goodman and I am a sophomore at Mount Saint Mary's University. I am a computer science and cyber security major. I have been taking private music lessons for about 15 years. I starting learning how to play the drums when I was 5 and the guitar when I was 7. I have always enjoyed playing music and I have a lot of experience both performing and teaching.

Music Experience

I have been in many different music ensembles throughout school. I was in the drumline and percussion section at Tuscarora High School. In high school, I competed in several competitions for drumline and jazz band. I also performed in countless concerts in 7 different ensembles. Outside of school, I have performed in many open mics and other concerts hosted by private lesson teachers. My experience in teaching music comes from being a leader for both the percussion section and drumline at my high school. I have taught in both group and one-on-one environments, and I also took 3 music theory classes in high school.







Why Make This Website?

Even though I take private lessons for the guitar, I use a lot of free online tools to get extra help and learn more than I am able to in lessons. There are a lot of free ways to learn the guitar online, but a lot of websites only focus on one thing. Some websites give written tutorials on how to play the guitar while others provide music and exercises to learn. I wanted to make a website that gives beginners a place to learn how to play the guitar where they can also practice and learn some starting exercises.
